Are all the tools on this website free to use?

Yes, absolutely! All tools available on EveryTool.Tech are completely free to use. There are no hidden charges, subscription fees, or usage limits.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No, you do not need to create an account. All our tools are accessible to everyone without requiring any registration or sign-up. Just visit the page and start using them right away.

How do you ensure the accuracy of the calculators?

We take accuracy very seriously. Our financial and health calculators are built based on standard, publicly available formulas and are thoroughly tested. However, they should be used for estimation and informational purposes only and not as a substitute for professional advice.
