Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tools and services

What is EveryTool.tech?

EveryTool.tech is a comprehensive platform that provides a wide range of online tools to help you with various tasks. From text manipulation to file conversion, we offer tools that make your work easier and more efficient.

Are the tools free to use?

Yes, all our basic tools are completely free to use. We also offer premium features for advanced users who need additional functionality.

How do I use the tools?

Using our tools is simple:

  • Browse through our collection of tools
  • Select the tool you need
  • Follow the on-screen instructions
  • Get your results instantly

Is my data safe?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All data processing is done locally in your browser when possible, and we never store your files or information on our servers unless explicitly required for the tool's functionality.

Can I suggest new tools?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new tools. You can submit your ideas through our contact form or by reaching out to our support team.
