Frequently Asked Questions

                
Quick answers about Open Graph and Twitter Card tags.

            

            

                

                    

                        Why do I need both Open Graph and Twitter Card tags?
                        
                    

                    

                        
Open Graph tags power previews on Facebook, LinkedIn, Slack, and many others. Twitter uses its own tag set. Adding both ensures your links look great no matter where they are shared.

                    

                


                

                    

                        What image size works best for social previews?
                        
                    

                    

                        
Use images at least 1200×630 pixels (aspect ratio ~1.91:1). This size keeps your preview sharp for both Open Graph and large Twitter cards on high-resolution screens.

                    

                


                

                    

                        Can I customize the Twitter card style?
                        
                    

                    

                        
Yes. This generator defaults to summary_large_image, which delivers the richest preview. Switch to summary if you prefer a smaller card without an image.

                    

                


                

                    

                        Do the tags update social previews instantly?
                        
                    

                    

                        
Some platforms cache previews. After updating your tags, use sharing debuggers (like Facebook Sharing Debugger or Twitter Card Validator) to refresh the cache and pull the latest data.
