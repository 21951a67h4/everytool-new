A Guide to Understanding Text Casing

Learn the rules behind different text cases and when to use them effectively.

What is Text Casing and Why Does It Matter?

Text casing refers to the capitalization of letters in a piece of text. Proper casing is crucial for readability, conveying the right tone, and adhering to grammatical standards. For example, using all uppercase can feel like shouting, while proper title case looks professional in headlines.

Common Types of Text Case

  • UPPERCASE: All letters are capitalized. GREAT FOR HEADLINES THAT NEED TO GRAB ATTENTION.
  • lowercase: all letters are small. often used for a stylistic, informal tone.
  • Sentence case: The first letter of the first word is capitalized. Standard for most sentences.
  • Title Case: The first letter of each major word is capitalized. Used for Titles of Books and Articles.
