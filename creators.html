Welcome

Hi, I’m Varshith — a full-stack developer who enjoys building practical and user-friendly projects. I believe in thinking from a user’s perspective, which helps me design tools that are simple, useful, and accessible to everyone.

EveryTool.tech, a free online platform that brings together helpful tools for everyday tasks. I created it to share my work with the community and make technology more approachable for everyone.

About Me

My interest in coding began during the COVID-19 lockdown, when I started exploring how websites are built. What began as curiosity soon became a real passion for creating digital products that solve everyday problems.

Building EveryTool.tech has been my most valuable learning experience. It taught me more than just programming — I learned project management, and how to turn ideas into live, working solutions.

My vision is to keep EveryTool.tech free for everyone, supported in the future by ads if needed. At the same time, I’m constantly learning new technologies, and I am currently focusing on Artificial Intelligence to prepare for my next big project.
