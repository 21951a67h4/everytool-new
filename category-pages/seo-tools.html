Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our SEO Tools

Are these free SEO tools a replacement for paid tools like Ahrefs or Semrush?

Our free tools are excellent for quick analysis, daily tasks, and learning SEO. They provide accurate, actionable data. However, comprehensive paid tools offer more extensive historical data, project management features, and deeper competitive analysis.

How accurate is the data from the Keyword Research Tool?

Our Keyword Research Tool uses data from Google Autocomplete and other public sources to generate relevant long-tail keywords and ideas. It's designed to give you a strong directional sense of what users are searching for.

How does the Website SEO Analyzer work?

The analyzer crawls a specific URL and checks it against a checklist of over 100 on-page and technical SEO best practices, including meta tags, page speed, content structure, and mobile-friendliness, providing a prioritized list of improvements.

Can I check my competitor's backlinks with your Backlink Checker?

Yes, our Backlink Checker is designed for competitive analysis. You can enter any domain to see its backlink profile, which can help you identify new link-building opportunities for your own website.

Do I need to be an SEO expert to use these tools?

No, not at all. Our tools are designed to be user-friendly, providing clear results and actionable recommendations that are easy for beginners to understand and implement.
