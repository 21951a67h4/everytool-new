Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Utility & Data Tools

Are utility tools safe for sensitive data?

Yes, all utility operations are performed locally in your browser. Sensitive data like passwords and personal information is never sent to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What types of data processing can these tools handle?

Our utility tools can generate secure passwords, hash data, encode/decode text, create charts and graphs, process CSV data, generate random data, and perform various data transformations.

Can I use these tools for business or commercial purposes?

Yes, all our utility tools are free to use for both personal and commercial purposes. There are no restrictions on usage for business applications.

Do these tools work offline?

Most utility tools work offline once the page is loaded, as they perform operations locally in your browser. However, some features may require internet connectivity for additional functionality.

Can I export or save the results from these tools?

Yes, most tools provide options to copy results to clipboard, download files, or save data in various formats. Export options vary by tool functionality.
